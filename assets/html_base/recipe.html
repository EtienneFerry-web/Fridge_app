format_list_numbered Method

1

Toast the Pine Nuts

Preheat your oven to 200°C (390°F). Thoroughly wash the carrots, leaving some of the green stems for an organic aesthetic. If making the pasta version, heat a small dry skillet over medium heat. Add pine nuts and toast.

2

Blend the Flavors

Toss the ingredients in a large bowl with wild honey, olive oil, and sea salt until evenly glazed. Arrange them on a baking tray in a single layer. Alternatively, pulse pesto ingredients in a food processor.

3

Cook & Combine

Roast for 35-40 minutes until tender and caramelized. Scatter fresh thyme in the last 5 minutes. If boiling pasta, cook until al dente, reserve water, and toss with pesto.
